About Me

Hello! I'm Piyush Sahoo, based in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India. I consider myself an engineer who is constantly learning engineering—not just as an academic discipline, but as a mindset for understanding how the world works and how it can be improved. Engineering for me is not limited to machines, software, or formulas; it is a way of thinking about systems, solving problems, and creating meaningful impact. My interests revolve around artificial intelligence, engineering, programming, entrepreneurship, product building, and modern technology. I am fascinated by how technology is shaping the future of humanity. Artificial intelligence in particular has enormous potential to transform industries, automate complex tasks, and amplify human creativity. At the same time, engineering principles provide the structure needed to build reliable and impactful solutions. One of my strongest motivations is a simple idea: to serve the planet. I believe technology should exist not only for convenience or profit but also to make life better for people and solve real-world problems. When used responsibly, technology can improve education, increase accessibility to knowledge, and empower individuals to do more than ever before. Alongside technology, I strongly value volunteering, open-source contributions, and teaching. I believe knowledge becomes far more powerful when it is shared. Teaching others helps communities grow and also deepens our own understanding. Beyond technical education, I also care about teaching moral values, responsible thinking, and character development. Technology without ethics can create serious problems, so combining innovation with strong values is extremely important. I also run a platform where I write about technology in simple and understandable ways. Modern tools, especially artificial intelligence, can often feel complex or intimidating. Through my articles and guides, I aim to simplify these ideas and help people learn how to use modern technology effectively. My goal is to make advanced concepts accessible so that more people can benefit from the digital world. Currently, I am learning and exploring multiple areas including Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, web development, business operations, and building a balanced spiritual lifestyle. I believe real growth requires development in multiple dimensions—technical knowledge, practical thinking, discipline, and mental clarity. Learning about business also helps me understand how ideas can turn into real products that serve people. I enjoy experimenting with different technologies and ideas, sometimes simply exploring them with curiosity and creativity. This experimental mindset allows me to constantly learn, improve, and discover new possibilities. Innovation often comes from curiosity and exploration rather than rigid structures. Personally, I try to cultivate qualities such as being a hard believer, maintaining a positive mindset, staying calm under pressure, remaining eager to learn, and being relentless in the pursuit of improvement. Challenges are a natural part of growth, and persistence is often the key factor that determines long-term success. One principle I strongly believe in is that hard work can beat almost anything. Talent and intelligence are valuable, but consistent effort and determination often matter even more. Progress may be slow sometimes, but continuous learning and discipline eventually lead to meaningful results. Another belief that guides my journey is that technology can make this planet a better one. When used thoughtfully, technology can empower people, expand access to knowledge, and create solutions that improve millions of lives. I want to contribute to this positive transformation by building, learning, teaching, and sharing ideas. For me, the journey of engineering and technology is not just about building tools—it is about understanding the world deeply and using that understanding to create something meaningful for others.
